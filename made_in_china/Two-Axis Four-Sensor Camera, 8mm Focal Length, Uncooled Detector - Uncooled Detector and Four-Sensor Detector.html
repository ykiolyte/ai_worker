    Product Description

    Product Introduction

     

    Introducing the FLYIR-S-120-2-F-POD gimbal, the epitome of advanced imaging technology, brought to you by Hebei Flyir Technology Co., Ltd.This innovative cameraboasts an uncooled infrared thermal imager paired witha versatile long and short-focus visible camerafeaturing12x hybrid zoom capability, alongside a precise laser ranging machine, mounted on a sophisticated two-axis servo-stabilized platform, with cutting-edge image processing components for autonomous recognition and tracking. It is distinguished by its ultra-small size, feather-light weight, impeccable precision, and minimal latency. Tailored for integration with small and micro unmanned aerial vehicles, it excels in conducting relentless, round-the-clock security and surveillance operations across targeted areas.
    The Gimbal Camera is engineered to detect,recognize,and monitor a variety of ground-based targets continuously for 24 hours, regardless of whether it's day or night. Additionally, it supports seamlessinfrared and visible light video streaming, all in real time.
    This state-of-the-art gimbal camera has been seamlessly integrated with numerous mainstream domestic flight control platforms, ensuring flawless docking with the flight control systems.Itis compatible with the comprehensive ViewControlStudio display and control software platform, which aids in expediting the development process of the unmanned aircraft system for overall unit efficiency.
    Its versatile application spectrum spans across security and surveillance,border patrol, search and rescue missions, as well as forest fire prevention efforts .
    Product Functions

     

    a) Self-check and fault report.

    c) 12.5x hybrid zoom.

    e) Optical zoom, autofocus, manual focus and low illumination.

    h) Auto search/manual search/servo/attitude stabilization/tracking.
    i) Receive instructions from the control station and complete the operations accordingly.
    j) Isolate the disturbances of the carrier, stabilize the line of sight.
    k) Lock/unlock the target, output the image with a tracking frame after the target is locked.
    l) Auto track the target and resist interference.
    m) Memory tracking with rapid target reacquisation.
    n) Adjust size of the wave gate.
    o) Switch tracking point.
    p) Calculate target coordinates from laser ranging and UAV information.
    q) Outputs infrared images, visible light images, system working status, camera working status and optical axis position through RS232/100Mbps Ethernet with the control station.
    r) Photo taking and video recording
