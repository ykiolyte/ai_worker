# MVP Limitations

The MVP intentionally excludes:

- autonomous purchasing;
- order confirmation;
- payments;
- mass messaging;
- advanced CRM;
- supplier scoring;
- advanced duplicate resolution across search requests;
- long-running negotiation automation;
- ERP/1C integration;
- Excel/CSV export;
- multi-tenant or advanced permission model;
- fine-tuning.

The agent may research products, normalize product cards, and send a first
supplier inquiry. It must not commit to purchases, promise payment, transmit
payment data, disclose secrets, or perform unauthorized actions.
